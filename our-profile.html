About

Telkom University is determined to become a research and entrepreneurial university that is beneficial to society. In line with this, we hope to be able to produce graduates who have global competitiveness and are able to create a culture of research and innovation that is useful in improving the quality of life of society, furthermore, able to contribute to national economic development through the development of entrepreneurship.

Vision

To become National Excellence Entrepreneurial University by 2028 that contribute to the fulfillment of sustainable development goals.

Mission

To become National Excellence Entrepreneurial University by 2028 that contribute to the fulfillment of sustainable development goals.
